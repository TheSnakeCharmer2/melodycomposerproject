This program allows people to create music with the help of javascript.

Users can drag notes around the staff and have the ability to play their creation back to them.

Melody composer is powered by VexFlow, which is used to program notes on a staff.

Melody Composer is tailored to make it so that a user may create their

own musical works and share them with others.
